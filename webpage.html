HOW TO MAKE LEAMONADE YOURSELF

Hi everyone , today we are gonna make a leamonade . I will guide you thru a couple of steps in order to make leamonade


so first of all grab all of the following ingredients : -

  1. water
  2. sugar
  3. lemons
  4. squeezer

Alright hope you have grabbed them now lets start the preparation follow the steps which leads you into making


  • Slice the lemons in half.
  • Squeeze them into a large measuring cup. A citrus juicer or squeezer makes this process easier. There should be about 1 1/2 cups of lemon juice to make 2 quarts of lemonade.

  • Remove any seeds , this is an important step
    • Do it carefully

  • Combine the lemon juice and sugar

  • stir into the cold water
    • we are nearing the end
    • Add any decoration you want , use your creativity

  • Serve over ice!!!!.

And there you go !! drink it whith your freinds and family
